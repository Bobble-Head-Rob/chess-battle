How to Play Quick rules
  • Pick pieces from the shop and place them in the blue deployment zone.
  • Stay within budget, then press Start Battle.
  • Pieces act by initiative; faster pieces act more often.
  • Ranged pieces fire from position. Knights strike in an L-shape and return if the target survives.
  • Pawns move straight forward and attack diagonally.
  • Destroy the enemy army. Score depends on result, actions, budget, and losses.
